Q&A

What to do if our attendance percentage(%) is low?

If your attendance percentage is above 75 percent, you are eligible to appear for semester Examinations. If you have attedance percentage above 60 percent, you have to pay fine to appear for examinations. If neither of the above, you have to provide proper reasons such as medical certificate.

what to do if our CAT marks are very low ?

You can ask the faculty to conduct additional test to improve the student's performance. You should also focus more on assignments to improve your internals.

Which is the right time to prepare for placement?

You enter the department at the start of Second year. It is also a right times to start your placements prepartion

what are all the website to prepare for placement?

Refer Competetive programming websites such as Leetcode, HackerRank etc. for coding preparations. For Aptitude Preparation refer indiabix.

What about the NPTEL Courses ?

You should complete 2 NPTEL courses spanning a total of 4 credits. The courses you take should not be part of your curriculum. And it is also mandatory to do atleast one HSM course either through NPTEL Or OEC.

What are OEC courses?

Open Elective Courses(OEC) are courses offered by other departments. You can apply for OEC courses starting from 5th Semester It is mandatory to complete 2 OEC courses. You can also take HSM courses in OEC.

What is atal incubation center, How to use it effectively ?

AIM is a flagship initiative to promote the culture of innovation and entrepreneurship in India. It is an umbrella structure that has been mandated to promote innovation and revolutionize the entrepreneurial ecosystem in the country. If you have any good entrepreneurial ideas, you can approach Atal incubation centre and get funding for it.

How to use library effectively?/features of our library ?

Our College library is equipped with a large number of books and online materials. Communicate with the library faculties to make best use of the library.

What will happen in the 8th Semester?

At the 8th Semester, you will only have project works. If you have got placed in any company, you would have started attending their training program.
